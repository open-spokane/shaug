## Join Us at Our Next Meeting

**When:** First Tuesday of every month at 7:00 PM
**Where:** Denny's on North Division, Spokane

Our meetings are a mix of:
- **Presentations** - Members showcase their setups, dashboards, and automation projects
- **Discussions** - Troubleshoot issues and share tips and tricks
- **Workshops** - Hands-on learning sessions for new features and integrations
- **Networking** - Connect with other smart home enthusiasts in the area

All experience levels are welcome! Whether you're curious about getting started with Home Assistant or you're looking to dive deep into advanced automations, there's a place for you here.
